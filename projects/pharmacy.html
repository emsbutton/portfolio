After the successful launch of the newly-redesigned Health Science Center website, my team immediately shifted to adapt the new design to each of the five health college websites.

I was tasked with creating a new set of components and pages that reflected the aesthetic of the new Health Science Center site, while also meeting the unique needs of each college. Here I’m presenting the College of Pharmacy’s redesign as a representation of my process for each college.
