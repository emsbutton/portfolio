Texas A&M Patient Care is the clinical care component of Texas A&M Health.

This website, like the clinical care concept itself, took many different forms before its current state and continues to evolve. Given only a name and a deadline, my team was asked to conceptualize a website that would tie together all of the universityâ€™s clinical care endeavors under the new brand.

We had several broad concepts but very little in the way of actual content. The design needed to resemble the streamlined cousin of the Health Science Center website: attractive, straightforward and functional with a focus on patient needs.

To achieve these goals and attempt to leave room for future growth and change, I drastically reduced the number of fonts, colors, and visual design elements in the flagship design. I put provider search and contact information front and center, and chose images that focus on positive patient-provider interactions. As Texas A&M Health continues to add clinic locations and patient resources, the site will be ready to expand to meet these demands.
